The Fat Flash Mob:

Creating ways to express herself as a performer and activist, Kathryn organized The Fat Flash Mob. With co-choreographer Alana Jane, she created several flash mobs to be danced on the same day in Los Angeles, California and Miami, Florida and Boston, Massachusetts on the same day.

A documentary film capturing The Fat Flash Mob, directed by Julie Wyman, has been seen in Sebastopol Documentary Film Festival, DocsDF in Mexico City, Atlanta DocuFest and it’s premier at SF DocFest’s Shorts 3: Bad Ass Women. You may view it at this link.
